## 🏛️ The Mission
VerifyZA is a comprehensive civic infrastructure designed to solve the "Trust Deficit" in South Africa. By providing instant verification of professional credentials and automating municipal accountability, we create a transparent ecosystem for citizens and small businesses.

---

## 📱 Mobile-First Economy
We are scaling beyond the web. The **VerifyZA Mobile App** (Play Store/iOS) is currently in community testing, designed specifically for low-data environments and featuring:
- 📶 **Offline Verification** for critical credentials.
- 🗣️ **Multilingual Support** in all 11 official South African languages.
- 🖨️ **Bluetooth Connectivity** for street vendors to print smart receipts.

---

## 🎯 The Three Pillars

### 1. 🔍 National Verification Layer
A reliable cross-reference tool for professional accreditations.
- **HPCSA** (Medical Professionals)
- **SAQA** (Qualifications & Universities)
- **DBE/SACE** (Schools & Educators)
- **CIPC** (Business Registrations)
- **CIDB** (Construction & Contractors)

### 2. 🏛️ MuniFix (Civic Reporting)
A community-driven portal to report bad services and infrastructure failure.
- **Automated Claims** for water, power, and road issues.
- **Urgency Scoring** mathematically calculated to prioritize reports.
- **Community Impact Tracking** to see real change in your neighborhood.

### 3. 🏪 SpazaAI (Business Intelligence)
Professional accounting and growth estimation for the spaza economy.
- 📊 **Mathematical Growth Estimates** based on local market trends.
- 📉 **Loss Prevention Analysis** to identify shop leakage.
- 🧾 **SARS Turnover Tax Engine** for micro-business compliance.

## 🏗️ Architecture & Stack
Built as a highly scalable monorepo ensuring professional grade security and performance.

| Layer       | Technology                              |
|-------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Frontend** | Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind, Framer Motion |
| **Backend**  | FastAPI (Python 3.11), Pydantic v2      |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL 16 + pgvector               |
| **Engine**   | Advanced RAG + Context-Aware Logic      |
| **Security** | JWT (RS256), Rate Limiting, RBAC       |
